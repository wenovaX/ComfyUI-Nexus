[h]Practical Tips (Must-Know)[/h]

→ First, verify whether the model is [b]SDXL or SD1.5[/b] on its page.
  - The most accurate way is to check the description or tags.
  - If there is no information, you can guess by the file size.
    ([b]SD1.5 is usually 2-4GB[/b], while [b]SDXL models are usually 5-7GB or larger[/b]. Use this as a reference, as exceptions may exist depending on the model.)

→ Some models with specially highlighted borders or icons may be paid or require additional conditions (subscriptions, credits, etc.).

→ Some models require a separate VAE file.
  - This is usually mentioned in the description or the download panel.

→ Intuitively rename your files when downloading.
  - Example: [b]sdxl_real_modelname_v1.safetensors[/b]
  - This saves a massive amount of time when searching through your files later.

→ Keep the [b]preview image[/b] together with the model file.
  - Saving it with the exact same name makes it extremely easy to manage as a thumbnail later.
