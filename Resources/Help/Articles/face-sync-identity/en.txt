[h]Face Sync: Fixing the Character's Face[/h]

Are you struggling because the main character's face changes every time you generate an image?
The technology to consistently apply a specific person's face to all generated images is called **'Face Sync'**.

[accent]💡 Note: Because this technology requires installing an additional library (InsightFace) and downloading dedicated models, it is considered a [b]'Master-class Advanced Quest'[/b] with a slightly higher entry barrier for beginners.
We highly recommend trying this out comfortably after you get fully accustomed to basic image generation![/accent]

[h]3 Essential Ingredients for the Exploration[/h]

To build this magic yourself, you must collect the following 3 core ingredients and place them in their correct directories:

[b]1. CLIP Vision Model (AI's Second Eye)[/b]
→ [b]Collectible:[/b] Explore and find the version that matches your base model—either [b]ViT-H[/b] for SD 1.5 or [b]ViT-bigG[/b] for SDXL—and place it in the [folder:ComfyUI/models/clip_vision|models/clip_vision] path.
→ [link:🔗 CLIP Vision Exploration Starting Point|https://huggingface.co/h94/IP-Adapter/tree/main/models]

[b]2. IP-Adapter FaceID Weights (Weights/Presets)[/b]
→ [b]Collectible:[/b] Preset files that map the source face's shape. Similarly, these are divided into SD 1.5 and SDXL versions.
→ [b]Path:[/b] Place in the [folder:ComfyUI/models/ipadapter|models/ipadapter] path.
→ [b]Exploration Hint:[/b] There is a historic mystery where some custom loader nodes only recognize specific extensions (e.g. [b]`.bin` extension[/b]), so caution is required during collection.
→ [link:🔗 FaceID Weights Exploration Starting Point|https://huggingface.co/h94/IP-Adapter-FaceID/tree/main]

[b]3. InsightFace Analysis Model (buffalo_l)[/b]
→ [b]Collectible:[/b] An invisible detector engine that precisely measures facial contours and exact coordinates of facial features from the photo. The [b]'buffalo_l'[/b] model—offering the highest detection accuracy—is the gold standard, and it generally builds and downloads automatically in the background on first run.
